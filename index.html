Current Project

Chickey-Checker-5000


For my most recent project I built an IoT device using the Particle Photon that checks conditions in my chicken coop and reports them to a webApp dashboard. It can track conditions such as temperature, humidity, heat index, and light levels currently. The webApp also has functionality to send out an alert via text or email when certain user-defined conditions are met (E.G. Temperature is below 32 Â°F). I'm working to give it functionality to track water and food levels, eggs laid, and control of the chicken coop heat lamp. I created this app using React.js, Redux.js, Redux-Saga, and Material UI for the frontend. Node.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL for the backend. I used Nodemailer, and Twillio to send alerts and the Particle cloud to get my device talking to the webapp.
